About VanRakshak

Our mission and technology

Our Mission

VanRakshak ("Forest Protector") is dedicated to using cutting-edge technology to detect and prevent forest fires before they cause devastating damage to our precious ecosystems.

How It Works

Our platform uses thermal satellite imagery to monitor forest temperatures in real-time. When abnormal temperature increases are detected, our system automatically generates alerts for fire agencies and local authorities, allowing for rapid response and intervention.

Key Features

  • Real-time thermal mapping of forest regions
  • Automated alert system for temperature anomalies
  • Historical data analysis for identifying high-risk zones
  • Mobile notifications for field personnel
  • Comprehensive analytics dashboard
